Welcome to Graina Bulk Food Store!

Graina is a bulk food store that brings you a personalised, no-waste shopping experience like none other in the market. We offer a wide selection of organic muesli (paleo and vegan friendly), lentils and legumes, dried fruits, nuts and seeds, various superfoods, organic teas, chocolates, snacks, organic dried spices, and much more in Moonee Ponds, Melbourne and all over Australia. All the products are offered in Bulk Food concept, where you can buy as much or as little as you want.
