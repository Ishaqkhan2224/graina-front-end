Packaging

NAKED FOODS Foods is passionate about the environment. As we sell bulk food and goods please be aware that most of our items are not conventionally packaged.

All our products hand packaged using either of the following ways:

All dry items (nuts, grains, muesli etc) is first packaged in Cellulose based plastic to reduce the chances of natural oil dispensation.
Cellulose is decomposable material and does so in 30-60 days.
After cellulose packaging, a paper packaging is used to provide further support.
All oils and liquids are packed using glass bottles.
Nut butters can be packaged using bio-decomposable plastic packaging.
